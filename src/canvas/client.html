Render contract

Shell-aware widget markup

Hiro can render semantic HTML directly into the canvas without recreating the whole page chrome.

  • Use canvas-report, canvas-grid, canvas-panel, and canvas-badge-row for structured layouts.
  • Wrap large tables in canvas-table-wrap so they stay readable on smaller screens.
  • Use inline <script> tags for calculators, toggles, or custom interactions.
